## Supported Tools

- **Calendly** - Meeting scheduling
- **Google Calendar** - Calendar management
- **Cal.com** - Open source scheduling

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    From dashboard, click **Integrations** → **Connectors**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Calendar Tool">
    Select Calendly, Google Calendar, or Cal.com connector
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy Connector">
    Deploy it as an MCP server and authenticate
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Agent">
    Go to your agent's **Tools** tab → **Add Tool** → Select the calendar connector
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your agent can now schedule meetings, check availability, and manage appointments!

## Use Cases

- Book meetings automatically
- Check calendar availability
- Send meeting reminders
- Reschedule appointments
