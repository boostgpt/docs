## Supported CRMs

- **HubSpot** - All-in-one CRM
- **Salesforce** - Enterprise CRM
- **Pipedrive** - Sales pipeline

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    From dashboard, click **Integrations** → **Connectors**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose CRM">
    Select HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive connector
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy Connector">
    Deploy it as an MCP server and authenticate with your CRM
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Agent">
    Go to your agent's **Tools** tab → **Add Tool** → Select the CRM connector
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your agent can now create contacts, update deals, and manage your CRM automatically!

## Use Cases

- Auto-create contacts from leads
- Update deal stages
- Log conversation notes
- Sync subscriber data
