## Supported Tools

- **Mailchimp** - Email marketing
- **SendGrid** - Transactional email
- **Gmail** - Email management

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    From dashboard, click **Integrations** → **Connectors**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Email Tool">
    Select Mailchimp, SendGrid, or Gmail connector
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy Connector">
    Deploy it as an MCP server and authenticate
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Agent">
    Go to your agent's **Tools** tab → **Add Tool** → Select the email connector
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your agent can now send emails, manage contacts, and automate email campaigns!

## Use Cases

- Send welcome emails to new subscribers
- Automate follow-up sequences
- Manage email lists
- Send transactional emails
