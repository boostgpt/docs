## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable">
    Go to **Settings** tab inside your agent and enable lead capture
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Timing">
    Ask after 3 messages (recommended)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Edit the message: "Share your email to get more info"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## View Leads

Go to **Subscribers** tab inside your agent to see captured emails and export CSV.

## Connect Tools

Send leads to Mailchimp, HubSpot, etc. See [Integrations](/creators/integrations/overview).
