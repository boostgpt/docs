## Add Training Content

Go to the **Memory** tab inside your agent to add training content.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upload Files">
    Upload PDF, Word, or text files (up to 500 KB on free plan)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Add URLs">
    Paste website URLs to extract content automatically
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Paste Text">
    Copy and paste FAQs or information directly
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## What to Include

- Company information
- Product/service details
- Top 10 FAQs
- Pricing and policies

## Best Practices

✅ Start with 5-10 key FAQs
✅ Be specific and clear
✅ Update content monthly
❌ Don't add irrelevant content
❌ Don't use vague answers
