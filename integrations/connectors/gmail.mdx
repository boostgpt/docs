## Overview

Connect Gmail to send, read, and manage emails through your agent.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    **Dashboard → Integrations → Connectors** → Find **Gmail** → **Deploy**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add to Agent">
    Agent → **Tools** tab → **Add Tool** → Select **Gmail**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure">
    Sign in with Google and grant Gmail permissions (OAuth)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test">
    Playground: "Send an email to john@example.com saying hello"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example Usage

```text
User: "Send an email to sarah@company.com with subject 'Meeting Tomorrow'
       and say we'll meet at 2pm"

Agent: I've sent the email to sarah@company.com
```

## Integration Example

**With HubSpot:**

```text
User: "Find all emails from leads in HubSpot and reply to them"

Agent: *searches HubSpot for leads*
       *searches Gmail for their emails*
       *sends personalized replies*

       Sent 5 follow-up emails to HubSpot leads
```
