## Overview

Connect Google Drive to upload, download, and manage files.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    **Dashboard → Integrations → Connectors** → Find **Google Drive** → **Deploy**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Agent">
    Agent → **Tools** tab → **Add Tool** → Select **Google Drive**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Sign in with Google and grant Drive access (OAuth)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test">
    Playground: "List my recent files"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example Usage

```text
User: "Upload the report.pdf to my Documents folder"

Agent: Uploaded report.pdf to Google Drive/Documents
```

## Integration Example

**With Gmail:**

```text
User: "Save all email attachments from today to Google Drive"

Agent: *gets Gmail attachments*
       *uploads to Drive*

       Saved 8 attachments to Google Drive
```
