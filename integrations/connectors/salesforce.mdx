## Overview

Connect Salesforce to manage leads, opportunities, accounts, and automate enterprise sales processes.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    **Dashboard → Integrations → Connectors** → Find **Salesforce** → **Deploy**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add to Agent">
    Agent → **Tools** tab → **Add Tool** → Select **Salesforce**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure">
    Sign in to Salesforce and grant CRM permissions (OAuth)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test">
    Playground: "Show me all open opportunities"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example Usage

```text
User: "Find all opportunities closing this quarter worth over $50K"

Agent: Found 8 opportunities:
       1. Acme Corp - $75K - Proposal/Price Quote
       2. TechStart - $120K - Negotiation/Review
       ...
       Total pipeline: $640,000
```

## Integration Example

**With Gmail:**

```text
User: "Create Salesforce leads from all emails marked 'New Lead'"

Agent: *searches Gmail for 'New Lead' label*
       *extracts contact information*
       *creates Salesforce leads*

       Created 7 new leads in Salesforce from Gmail
```
