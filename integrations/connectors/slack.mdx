## Overview

Connect Slack to send messages, manage channels, and automate team communication.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    **Dashboard ’ Integrations ’ Connectors** ’ Find **Slack** ’ **Deploy**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add to Agent">
    Agent ’ **Tools** tab ’ **Add Tool** ’ Select **Slack**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure">
    Sign in to Slack and authorize your workspace (OAuth)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test">
    Playground: "Send a message to #general saying hello"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example Usage

```text
User: "Post to #engineering that the deploy is complete"

Agent: Message sent to #engineering:
       "Deploy completed successfully at 3:45 PM"
```

## Integration Example

**With HubSpot:**

```text
User: "Post new HubSpot deals to #sales"

Agent: *checks HubSpot for new deals*
       *posts to Slack*

       Posted to #sales: "New deal: Acme Corp - $25K"
```
